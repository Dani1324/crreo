Cookies

Last updated December 18, 2022

Our website may use “cookies” to help personalize and enhance your online experience. Cookies are small text files, generally made up of letters and numbers, that are placed on your computer, tablet, mobile phone, or other device when you visit a web page. Cookies are uniquely assigned to you and can only be read by a web server in the domain that issued the cookie to you. Our Privacy Policy explains how we collect and use information from and about you when you use our website and other services. This policy explains more about how we use cookies and your related choices.

How We Use Cookies

Like most providers of web-based services, we may use cookies for a number of reasons, like protecting your data and any user accounts you may create by, for example, reducing the number of times you’re required to sign in and transmit your account credentials. We may also use cookies to help us understand how our website is accessed and used, count visitors to a page, improve our overall user experience, keep our web-based services secure, and provide you with a better, more intuitive, and satisfying overall user experience. The cookies we use generally fall into one of the following categories.

Type of Cookie Reasons for Use
Preferences We use these cookies to remember your settings and preferences, including, for example, sign-in preferences.
Security We use these cookies to help identify and prevent security risks. For example, we may use these cookies to store your session information and prevent others from changing your password without your username and password.
Performance We use these cookies to collect information about how you interact with our services to help us improve their functionality.

Your Ability to Control Cookies

Your browser or device may allow you to block or otherwise limit the use of cookies and it is within your rights to do so, but cookies are an important part of how our services (and most other online services) work. Restricting or limiting the use of cookies, whether specifically for our website or for all websites, could affect the availability and functionality of our services.

Browser Cookies

Your browser may provide you with the option to refuse some or all browser cookies. You may also be able to remove cookies from your browser. For more information about how to manage browser cookies, please follow the instructions provided by your browser.

Mobile Device Identifiers

Your mobile operating system may let you opt-out from having certain device identifiers used for interest-based advertising. You should refer to the instructions provided by your mobile device’s manufacturer; this information is typically available under the “settings” function of your mobile device.

If you have any questions about our use of cookies, if you believe we are using them in violation of your rights or preferences, or for general inquiries, please contact our Data Protection Officer at dpo@crreo.co. For more general inquiries, please contact us at hello@crreo.co.
