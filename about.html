Who we are

CRREO is a growth platform for creators. With CRREO, creators can grow their passion into a successful business.

We help content creators grow their income, build their brand, and win brand sponsorships.

We provide actionable growth insights and facilitate mentorship from industry leaders.

We help creators and their fans build more meaningful engagement focused on collaboration and growth.

MISSION

Our mission is to enable independent content creators to achieve their growth goals. We're working to redefine how independent quality content is funded, produced, and distributed.

We believe that the creator economy should primarily benefit the creators and their community, not large tech platforms. We believe that sponsorships and growth tools should be accessible to all creators, not just large media houses. We believe that creators can grow faster through closer collaboration with their fans.

CORE PRINCIPLES

1

We deliver unique and game-changing value to creators, and their fans, in everything we do.

2

We create a caring place to work, grow, learn, and have fun, for everyone on the team.

3

We build a company that inspires, improves people’s lives and makes us proud to be part of it.
